Makoto Shinkai 新海誠
  • Name: Makoto Shinkai (新海誠, Shinkai Makoto)
  • Occupation: Animator, Film-maker, Manga Artist
  • Nationality: Japan
  • Birthplace: Nagano Prefecture, Japan
  • Birth Date: Feb 09, 1978
  • Age: 51 Years, 8 Months
  • Originally: Makoto Niitsu

Makoto Shinkai (新海 誠, Shinkai Makoto), born as Makoto Niitsu (新津 誠, Niitsu Makoto), is a Japanese director, writer, producer, animator, editor, cinematographer, voice actor, manga artist and former graphic designer. He is best known for directing Your Name., one of the highest-grossing anime films of all time,[1] and was listed by Variety as one of 10 animators to watch in 2016.[2] Asteroid 55222 Makotoshinkai is named after him.
